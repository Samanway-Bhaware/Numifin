# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Founders, indie hackers, and small business owners who self-host their own bookkeeping stack. They use NumiFin during focused work sessions to review AI categorizations, check cash flow, reconcile ledgers, and ask their CFO agent questions. They are technically capable and privacy-conscious, not accountants by trade. The marketing landing page reaches this same audience before sign-up.

## Product Purpose

NumiFin is a self-hostable, privacy-first, AI-powered accounting SaaS built as a multi-agent financial intelligence platform. It ingests transactions, receipts, and invoices; categorizes and reconciles financial data through a pipeline of five specialized agents; generates deterministic financial insights; and explains every AI decision. Users supply their own OpenAI API key. No financial data leaves their infrastructure.

## Brand Personality

Precise, trustworthy, understated. Like a quiet expert in the room who only speaks when it matters. Three words: focused, capable, reserved.

## Anti-references

- Loud consumer fintech with neon gradients and performative numbers (Robinhood, Crypto.com, Coinbase)
- Generic SaaS dashboard cliches: identical blue metric hero cards, icon-grid features, gradient-text headlines
- AI products that perform intelligence theatrically instead of delivering it quietly

## Design Principles

1. Precision over performance: every element earns its place; no motion that exists only to be seen
2. Transparency by default: AI decisions are explained, not hidden behind confidence theater
3. Expert confidence: the interface speaks quietly and clearly, never boasts
4. Privacy demonstrated: self-hosted and user-controlled, reflected in restraint not flashiness
5. Financial gravity: numbers are primary, chrome is secondary

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Always respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Financial numbers must be unambiguous at all sizes and in all color contexts.
